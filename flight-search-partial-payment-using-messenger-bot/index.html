Flight search and partial payment UX using Messenger bot

I'm not a big fan of chatbots. However, this UX concept designed by Isil Uzum with a Virgin Atlantic Messenger bot is very clever.

The premise is this -- John and Kathy while chatting on Messenger, think of buying flight tickets. Kathy launches flight search using =flight search shortcut. This triggers the flight bot, Virgin Atlantic in this case. Kathy gets to choose between Skyscanner and Kayak search engines for flights. She even makes Skyscanner as the default search engine. Then, she chooses a flight from JFK to SEA along with to and from dates and makes it for 2 pax. Both John and Kathy get to fill in the passenger information and choose partial payment. Kathy only pays half the total price. When both John and Kathy finish paying, their ticket confirmation pass is shown in the chat itself.
