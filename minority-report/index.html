Minority Report

Wednesday, May 01, 2013

When Minority Report came in 2002, it had ultra-thin touch screens, gestures: swiping, pinch to zoom, Kinect / leap motion, Siri / Google Now like AI assistants, wearable computer gadgets like the  watch / Google Glass, self driving cars etc. Today, in 2014, we live in a world where all of that fantasized future we once had, is actually a reality.

I’m not really a Spielberg fan but I wonder whether he actually foresaw that the future would be something like this, or was this a purely coincidence and part of his researched script? If Spielberg did actually predict it right, well that’s really brilliant! I would want him to make Minority Report 2 as well, so that we can have a peek into our future once again.
I’ve seen other sci-fi movie directors, who took way too much liberty and introduced concepts like teleportation, time travel etc. I have a feeling that these things would remain a human fantasy for a long time and may not see the light.

There are also movies such as Aditya 369 or the very recent Oblivion, where after an apocalypse, the survivors would be seen wearing a silver colored pinstripe jump suit with boots! Actually, Jerry Seinfeld made a joke on this.
